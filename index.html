Bio

I am an aspiring web developer currently working my way through the Front End Web Development bootcamp at Thinkful. Coding is a source of great joy for me. I love having problems to solve and developing creative solutions to those problems in the pursuit of efficiency. Few things give me as deep a feeling of satisfaction as starting from nothing and crafting an elegant and accessible app.

I am coming to coding from a background in e-Commerce, where I was able to experience firsthand how critical intelligent design is to the success of a website. I want to leverage this passion for UI/UX optimization to make tools that improve the Web experience for everyone and bring fully realized ideas to people that will benefit their lives.

Beyond coding, my hobbies include music (performing, attending, listening), cooking, watching Bob’s Burgers, and hanging out with my tripod dog Pawlie.
